iTunesRichPresence

iTunesRichPresence is a tool to bring iTunes "Now Playing" information to Discord via Rich Presence.

After five years of uncertainty regarding the project's maintenance, I have made the final decision: iTunesRichPresence is no longer maintained or supported.

The last release will remain available at the link below for the foreseeable future. Please note that this version is provided as-is, with no support available.
